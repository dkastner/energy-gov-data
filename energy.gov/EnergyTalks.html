Energy Talks Video Series

Sunita Satyapal, Director of the Fuel Cell Technologies Office, in her talk "Hydrogen and Fuel Cell Revolution" speaks about the future of fuel cell technologies.

Kevin Lynn, Director of the Grid Modernization Initiative, speaks about the effort to reshape our nation's power grid.

David Anderson, Vehicle Technologies Office Technology Manager, discusses the SMART Mobility Initiative for energy efficient mobility systems.

Alison Goss Eng, Bioenergy Technologies Office Program Manager, speaks about the history of bioenergy and what the future holds for the growing field.

Erika Gupta, Fuel Cell Technologies Office Technology Development Manager, speaks to high schoolers about what fuel cell technology is all about.

Paul Basore, Sunshot Initiative Chief Engineer, talks about solar power in today's world.

Sam Rashkin, Building Technologies Office Chief Architect, speaks about the path to a sustainable future using power words.

Amir Roth, Building Technologies Office Modeling Technology Manager, speaks about building energy efficient buildings using revolutionary computer modeling software developed by DOE.

Crystal McDonald, Weatherization and Intergovernmental Programs Energy Policy Advisor, speaks to high school students about efforts to lower the energy usage of their school buildings.

Tony Bouza, Building Technologies Office Technology Manager, speaks about Magnetocaloric, a Zero GWP Solution.

Doug Hollett, Principal Deputy Assistant Secretary, Office of Fossil Energy, speaks about the fossil energy in a low carbon economy.

Don Macdonald, Senior Advisor for Strategic Projects, Office of Technology Transitions, in his talk titled, "Turning Ideas into Impact: Seven Decades of Commercialization at DOE", speaks about the many innovations brought to fruition by our national labs.

David Friedman, Former Acting Assistant Secretary of the Office of Energy Efficiency & Renewable Energy, speaks about accelerating a clean energy revolution.

Johanna Wolfson, Technology-to-Market Director in Office of Energy Efficiency and Renewable Energy, shares the importance of re-imagining the pathway from energy idea to commercialization.

Mark Johnson, Director of the Advanced Manufacturing Office, Office of Energy Efficiency and Renewable Energy, talks about the past and the future of American manufacturing.

Johanna Wolfson, Technology-to-Market Director in Office of Energy Efficiency and Renewable Energy, shares the importance of re-imagining the pathway from energy idea to commercialization.

EnergyTalks presentations provide in-depth explanations of the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) programs by energy specialists. Each 10-20 minute presentation highlights an aspect of our revolutionary impact on energy innovation. The complexity and breadth of this work is world-renowned and available in video format.
