#CleanTechNow: Your Best Clean Energy Photos

When it comes to clean energy, the future is already here -- and during our #CleanTechNow feature, you showed us how it's already playing a role in your daily lives. Below, check out highlights from #CleanTechNow and our favorite photos shared by energy.gov readers (including a solar-loving dog).

Storified by Energy Department ·
Fri, Sep 27 2013 16:37:15

It's often said that America's clean energy future will "always be five years away," Secretary Moniz wrote in a recent op-ed. "Today, that is changing." A new Energy Department report shows how the future is already here for four key clean energy technologies -- wind, solar PV, electric vehicles and LED lighting -- that in recent years have dropped in cost and surged in consumer demand.

America's #cleanenergy future isn't 5 years away. For 4 key technologies, it's already here: go.usa.gov/DNkC #CleanTechNow
