Former Multimedia Editor, Office of Public Affairs

Yesterday, Dr. Ernest Moniz was sworn in as the nation's 13th Secretary of Energy and met Department employees after the ceremony in the Forrestal cafeteria. Afterwards, Secretary Moniz made his first public speech at the 2013 Energy Efficiency Global Forum, where he spoke about the importance of energy efficiency and how advancing demand-side solutions and eliminating waste will be one of his top priorities. 

You can follow Secretary Moniz on Twitter (@ErnestMoniz) or on Facebook.
