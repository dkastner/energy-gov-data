A recap of the recent #SolarisNow Twitter Chat

Storified by Energy Department ·
Mon, May 05 2014 14:40:18

Last week, Peter Davidson, executive director of the Energy Department's Loan Programs Office answered questions on all things solar during @NRGEnergy's live #solarisnow Twitter Chat.

Join us for a Twitter Chat by using #SolarIsNow as we celebrate PV & the introduction of Agua Caliente, the world’s largest PV project.

— NRG Energy, Inc. ·
Tue, Apr 29 2014 17:59:09

Hi I’m Peter Davidson, Exec. Dir. of @Energy’s Loan Programs Office → http://t.co/vSZovYsuQL. Looking forward to chatting! #SolarisNow -PD

The discussion happened the same day as the launch of Agua Caliente -- the world's largest solar photovoltaic power plant.

Agua Caliente, world’s largest solar PV plant comes online → http://t.co/WnUOiN8Dxl #solarisnow http://t.co/QvKvSmmIPw ·

NRG Energy kicked off the solar chat with a question on solar success stories:

Q1: As the industry continues to thrive, what are the most impressive #solar success stories? #SolarIsNow

We shared details on the rapid growth of large-scale solar power plants across the country:

In 2009 largest US PV plant was 25MW. Then 5 100+MW plants were financed w/ LPO. Next 10 didn’t need @ENERGY financing. #solarisnow

Tue, Apr 29 2014 18:03:44

A1: Big plants like Ivanpah showing Big Solar is feasible, and solar leasing opening up residential solar #solarisnow

— Tom Overton ·

Another great LPO project is Solana: groundbreaking 6 hours of molten salt storage = nightime solar http://t.co/wY7iDnUfvj #solarisnow
