SmartBuy

Arrows

SmartBUY is a federal strategic sourcing initiative featuring blanket purchase agreements for commercial off the shelf software, designed to maximize the government's buying power and decrease the cost of widely used commercial software. The Department of Energy (DOE) fully supports and complies with the SmartBUY initiative.
