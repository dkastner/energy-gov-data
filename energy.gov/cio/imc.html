Information Management Conference

The 34th Information Management Conference (IMC) sponsored by the Office of the Chief Information Officer (OCIO) will be held at the Gaylord Opryland Convention Center in Nashville, TN, June 15-18, 2015. The conference theme is:

"Mission Excellence through Innovation"

The Office of the Chief Information Officer (OCIO) is committed to an effective, collaborative strategy with the Department’s Programs, Field, and Laboratory Elements. The IMC provides information exchange and training to federal and contractor staff.  The goal of the conference is to advance the Department’s mission through policy, standards, and services, which meet mission requirements, balance risk and innovation, and set clear performance goals and expectations for the information ecosystem. Furthermore, the conference is aligned with the Information Resources Management Strategic Plan vision, which aims to collaborate as an enterprise and deliver innovative information management and technology solutions that support the Department’s mission.

KEY DATES

Conference Registration:  February 23, 2015 - May 29, 2015 - Extended
Abstract (Call for Papers) Submission:  February 2, 2015 - April 10, 2015 (CLOSED)
Conference Housing Reservations:  March 6, 2015 - May 29, 2015 (11:59 p.m. EST)

AGENDA* http://www.energyimc.com/agenda
REGISTRATION http://www.energyimc.com/registration
HOTEL/TRAVEL INFORMATION www.energyimc.com/Travel/Venue
FAQ's www.energyimc.com/FAQs

* Please note this is a working agenda and subject to change.

Note: Prior to registering for the conference, please confirm that a slot is available with your primary Program POC. You can find your Program POC on the Conference Management Powerpedia page at https://powerpedia.energy.gov/wiki/Conference_Management_(DOE) under Conference POCs. The deadline to use allotted slots is May 1, if your program slots are not used by that date they will be released and offered to another program. If your program is out of slots and you'd like to be put on the waiting list, please contact IMC@hq.doe.gov. If you're getting a waitlist notification after registering, but you have been approved by your Conference POC to attend, please contact us. 

For additional information on the Information Management Conference including the agenda, please visit www.energyimc.com.

If you have any questions please contact the IMC Mailbox at IMC@hq.doe.gov

If you’re industry looking for partner/exhibit/industry information please see: http://www.energyimc.com/Exhibits/Prospectus

NOTE: Participation as a partner or exhibitor in the IMC does not imply any affiliation with or endorsement by the U.S. Department of Energy
