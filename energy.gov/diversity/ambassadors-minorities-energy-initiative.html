Ambassadors of the Minorities in Energy Initiative

The Minorities in Energy Initiative's Ambassadors partnership was established by Secretary Ernest Moniz and Director LaDoris Harris on November 13, 2013. The Ambassadors are comprised of  senior-level leaders whom are committed to lending their voice to the mission of the Minorities in Energy Initiative through outreach and strategic planning. The Ambassadors are passionate about engaging minorities in all aspects of the energy sector, with a special focus on energy economic development, STEM education, and climate change.
