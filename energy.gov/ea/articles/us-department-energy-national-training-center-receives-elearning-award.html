U.S. Department of Energy National Training Center Receives eLearning Award

February 9, 2015 - 10:53am

Addthis

U.S. Department of Energy National Training Center (NTC) won a prestigious eLearning award at the recent eLearning Guild DevLearn 2014 Conference and Expo, the leading training and learning event in North America focused on learning technologies.

The U.S. Department of Energy National Training Center (NTC) won a prestigious eLearning award at the recent eLearning Guild DevLearn 2014 Conference and Expo, the leading training and learning event in North America focused on learning technologies. The eLearning DemoFest, a collective eLearning showcase where conference participants present their latest projects, is a highlight of the conference. The DemoFest competition, held October 30th, featured 81 project demonstrations from around the world, including six NTC courses—Workplace Violence Awareness Briefing, Roles and Responsibilities for Key HRP Officials, Personnel Security Processing, Special Access Programs, Engagement Simulation Systems Demo, and Unusual Behavior. Of the many excellent submissions, the NTC’s Workplace Violence Awareness Briefing entry won the award for Best Immersive/Simulation. 

The Workplace Violence Awareness Briefing was designed to provide DOE Federal and contractor personnel with information on how to recognize and respond to warning signs of potential workplace violence, how to plan and prepare for a workplace violence incident, and how to recognize and respond correctly to a workplace violence incident. It leveraged the use of story in dramatic fashion by following two employees at the start of a workday in which an active shooter event takes place. Using a series of experts, the learner walks through various aspects of workplace violence, culminating in two interactive active shooter scenarios. These scenarios present the learner with options showing immediate consequences while providing instant expert feedback.

Rob Adelman, a Chenega Support Services employee and the eLearning Team Lead at the NTC, developed the Workplace Violence Awareness Briefing course and demonstrated it during the DemoFest competition. The development of the course was a collaborative team effort, including major contributions from Don DeLuca (video), Aaron Berger (video/audio), and Jeremy Montoya (graphics). Other contributions were made by team members Aaron Toth, Andrea Barela, Kathy Cairns, Rob Clark, Ezra Sandoval, John Bicknell, and Elaine Brown.

This highly esteemed award is voted on by peers, experts, and industry leaders in the eLearning field. This is the second year in a row that an NTC course has won an award at this conference.

The Workplace Violence Awareness Briefing can be found on the NTC Learning Management System under Insider Threat at https://lms.ntc.doe.gov/u/.
