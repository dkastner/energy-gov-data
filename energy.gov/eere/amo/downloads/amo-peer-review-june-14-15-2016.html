AMO PEER REVIEW, JUNE 14-15, 2016

The Advanced Manufacturing Office (AMO) conducted a review of its technology research, development, and demonstration (RD&D) activities in Rosslyn, VA, on June 14-15, 2016. The meeting was open to the public. Leading technology experts from the industrial, academic, and government sectors reviewed AMO's Program and provided their view on its effectiveness and impact. Read a letter from AMO’s director about the peer review. View the agenda.
