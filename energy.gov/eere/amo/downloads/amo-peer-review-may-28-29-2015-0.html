AMO PEER REVIEW, MAY 28-29, 2015

The Advanced Manufacturing Office (AMO) conducted a Peer Review of its Research, Development, and Demonstration (RD&D), Facilities, and Analysis activities in Washington, DC, on May 28-29, 2015. The meeting was open to the public. Leading technology experts from the industrial, academic and government sectors reviewed AMO's Program and provided their view on its effectiveness and impact. The Peer Review was held concurrently with the Better Buildings Summit. View the agenda.

The presentations provided at the Review are listed below.
