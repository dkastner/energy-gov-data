AMO Peer Review, May 6-7, 2014

The Advanced Manufacturing Office (AMO) conducted a review of its portfolio of Research and Development (R&D), Facilities, and Technology Assistance projects and activities in Washington, D.C. on May 6-7, 2014. The meeting was open to the public. Leading technology experts from the industrial and academic sectors reviewed AMO's R&D portfolio. View the agenda. Read the Letter from the AMO Director.
