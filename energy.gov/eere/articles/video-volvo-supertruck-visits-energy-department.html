Video: Volvo SuperTruck Visits Energy Department

September 15, 2016 - 11:31am

Addthis

Mike Mueller
Senior Digital Content Strategist, EERE Communications
Infographic: How SuperTruck is making heavy duty vehicles more efficient

Volvo Trucks North America’s SuperTruck recently showcased a number of its advanced technologies at the Energy Department. The SuperTruck I initiative aims to make Class 8 trucks more fuel efficient. Three teams - Volvo, Daimler Trucks North America, and Cummins & Peterbilt - have all achieved more than 50% freight efficiency compared to a 2009 baseline model. Navistar is also on track to meet that mark later in 2016. 

Watch the video above to see how the Energy Department is working to make Class 8 trucks more fuel-efficient. You can also visit our Vehicle Technologies Office website to learn more about what we’re doing to deploy sustainable highway transportation technologies that cut carbon pollution and drive economic growth.
