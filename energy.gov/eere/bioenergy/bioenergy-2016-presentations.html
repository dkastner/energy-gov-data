Bioenergy 2016: Presentations

This page displays the links to available presentations from  the Bioenergy Technologies Office's (BETO’s) Bioenergy 2016 conference. Approved presentations have been made available. Copying or using any materials without the consent of the presentation owner is prohibited.

Day One: Tuesday, July 12, 2016

Welcome and Introductory Keynotes

  • Jonathan Male, Director for the Bioenergy Technologies Office, U.S. Department of Energy
  • Reuben Sarkar, Deputy Assistant Secretary for Transportation, U.S. Department of Energy

Plenary I: Life, Liberty, and the Pursuit of Biomass

  • Matt Langholtz, Natural Resource & Environmental Economist,Oak Ridge National Laboratory
  • Laurence Eaton, Research Economist, Oak Ridge National Laboratory
  • David Sudolsky, Co-Founder and CEO, Anellotech, Inc.
  • Matt Carr, Executive Director, Algae Biomass Organization

Plenary II: Creating Commercial Success in the Bioeconomy

  • Brooke Coleman, Executive Director, Advanced Biofuels Business Council
  • Brian Foody, President and CEO, Iogen Corporation
  • K’Lynne Johnson, Board Director, FMC Corporation
  • Martin Sabarsky, CEO, Cellana, LLC
  • Taite McDonald, Senior Policy Advisor, Holland and Knight

Day One Keynote and Closing Remarks

  • Vann Bush, Managing Director, Gas Technology Institute
  • Jim Spaeth, Program Manager for Demonstration and Market Transformation, Bioenergy Technologies Office, U.S. Department of Energy

Welcome and Morning Keynotes

  • Jonathan Male, Director for the Bioenergy Technologies Office, U.S. Department of Energy
  • David Friedman, Acting Assistant Secretary, Energy Efficiency and Renewable Energy, U.S. Department of Energy
