Building Technologies Office 2015 Program Peer Review

The 2015 Department of Energy (DOE) Building Technologies Office (BTO) Peer Review was held April 14-16, 2015 in Vienna, Virginia. This third annual review encompassed many of BTO's active research, development, demonstration and deployment projects. Independent experts assessed the progress and contributions of each project toward BTO's mission and goals, and these assessments will be used to enhance the management of existing efforts, gauge the effectiveness of projects, and design future programs. The meeting also provides an opportunity to promote collaborations, partnerships, and technology transfers.

Please contact btopeerreview@ee.doe.gov with any questions
