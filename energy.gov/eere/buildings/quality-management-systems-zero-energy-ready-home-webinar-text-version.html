So what are some of the implications as you're making this transition that you have to keep in mind? You have to worry about air quality. Think about ventilation. You've got to think about combustion safety. You've got to think about using the right equipment to make sure that combustion safety is not an issue. You may introduce moisture problems into the walls if you're not doing it correctly. Your details and specifications potentially are probably going to be more complex. So the ISO 9001 systems, it's a comprehensive system. And it's a universal system. It applies to any business. It covers the entire business. The one takeaway from this chart that you see here, the circle that's in the middle, that's the engine. That's the company that's making the product.
