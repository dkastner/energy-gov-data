Get a Free Copy of the Energy Literacy Framework

Energy Literacy: Essential Principles and Fundamental Concepts for Energy Education presents interdisciplinary energy concepts that, if understood and applied, will help individuals and communities make informed energy decisions. Learn more about Energy Literacy.

Download a copy of Energy Literacy, available in both high- and low-resolution PDFs and as a Word document below.

If you would like to have hard copies of the guide sent to you by mail, please send an email to energyliteracy@ee.doe.gov with the following information:
- Name
- Address
- Phone number
- Organization/school (if applicable)
- Number of copies requested

Note: We only ship a minimum of 10 copies. Please indicate if you would like English and/or Spanish versions.

Need help applying the Energy Literacy principles and concepts to your classroom? Check out our Quick Start Guide.
