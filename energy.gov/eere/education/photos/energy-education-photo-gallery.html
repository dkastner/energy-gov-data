Independence High School Students visit Department of Energy.

These Independence High School students (CA) visited #DOE in July 2013 to learn about careers in #energy and #STEM. These students are part of the Pacific Gas & Electric Company New Energy Academy.

University of Maryland's First Energy 101 Class.

The University of Maryland at College Park was one of the first Universities to use Energy’s Energy 101 Course Framework in the Spring (2013). Pictured are students with their end of the year projects geared towards designing, engineering and energy consumption in a sustainable world.

Image: Photo courtesy of the University of Maryland.

AZ's PIMA County using Energy Literacy to Train Educators.

DaNel Hogan (pictured), a science teacher and former Department of Energy Albert Einstein Distinguished Fellow is a STEM coordinator in Arizona training teachers on the Energy Literacy Principles.

Image: Photo Courtesy of DaNel Hogan.

Geothermal Student Competition 2013 Winners.

Student team members Talor Walsh, Leah Sabbeth, and team leader David Brink-Roby from New York’s University of Rochester won the 2013 intercollegiate geothermal challenge.

Image: Photo courtesy of Geothermal Technologies Office.

Energy Literacy reaches EmPowerGirlz Program at YWCA NCA.

The YWCA National Capital Area and Department of Energy kicked off the EMPowERgirlz STEM 103: The World of Science day (2/22/2014) with the energy bike and magnetic building blocks exploring the seven Energy Literacy Principles

Churchhill Road Elementary Celebrates Earth Day with Energy Bike.
