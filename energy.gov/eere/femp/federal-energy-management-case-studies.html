The Federal Energy Management Program (FEMP) offers case studies featuring federal agencies that have successfully implemented energy- and water-efficient technologies and measures into their buildings and operations. Examples of the successful use of FEMP financing mechanisms are also available.

Explore case studies by clicking the topics below. FEMP publications are also available.
