Federal Renewable Energy Project Assistance

The Federal Energy Management Program (FEMP) offers resources to help agencies assess, identify, and finance renewable energy projects that meet federal laws and requirements.

View FEMP's additional options for agency assistance, including interagency partnerships. Information about project planning and implementation is also available.

Maps, Tools, and Technologies

Consider the Options

Start a project by researching renewable energy technologies. Then use FEMP’s renewable energy maps and tools to assess the viability of a project.

Financing and Procurement

Finance Your Project

There are several options available for financing renewable energy projects. Read about project financing options, including renewable energy procurement.

Training

Get Educated

FEMP’s live and on-demand training explains renewable energy requirements and how to implement projects.

Technical Assistance

Ask for Help

The FEMP Technical Assistance Request Portal allows federal agencies to request help with renewable energy projects.
