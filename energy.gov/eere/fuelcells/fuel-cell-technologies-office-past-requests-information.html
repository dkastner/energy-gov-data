Fuel Cell Technologies Office Past Requests for Information

The Fuel Cell Technologies Office periodically releases requests for information (RFIs) to solicit feedback from stakeholders on a variety of hydrogen and fuel cell-related topics. View past RFIs issued by the office. Open RFIs are listed on our funding opportunities page.
