2009

December 16, 2009
  • Topic: Connecticut Hydrogen and Fuel Cell Activities and the Regional Resource Center
  • Presenters from the Connecticut Center for Advanced Technology, Inc.:
    • Joel Rinebold, Director of Energy Initiative, gave an overview of Connecticut's Hydrogen and Fuel Cell Markets, Programs, and Models.
    • Tom Drejer, Energy Specialist, discussed the Regional Resource Center, which provides online information, models, and other tools to assist local and state planners to quantify the costs and benefits of hydrogen and fuel cell technology at identified potential sites.

    • Connecticut Fuel Cell Activities: Markets, Programs, & Models
    • Regional Resource Center: A Connecticut Center for Advanced Technology/US DOE Partnership Initiative
