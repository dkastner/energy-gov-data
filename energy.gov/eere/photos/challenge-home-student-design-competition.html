Challenge Home Student Design Competition

Addthis

1 of 10

Challenge Home Student Design Competition

Teams and judges participating in the Challenge Home Student Design Competition stand front of the LEED Platinum CAFE at the National Renewable Energy Laboratory in Golden, Colorado. The event, which took place April 26 and April 27, featured 28 collegiate teams presenting their cost-effective, zero energy ready home designs.

Image: Dennis Schroeder, National Renewable Energy Laboratory

2 of 10

Ryerson University

The Ryerson University team won the grand award in the single family attached category for their high-performance townhome design. Pictured are Building Technologies Office Chief Architect Sam Rashkin (from left), Ryerson team members Patrick Andres, Mark Grimsrud, Antonio Cunha, Matthew Tokarik, and Housing Innovation Research Laboratory Vice President of Engineering and Research Thomas Kenney. (Photo by Dennis Schroeder / NREL)

Image: Dennis Schroeder, National Renewable Energy Laboratory

3 of 10

HIGH PERFORMANCE DESIGN

Ryerson University won the grand award in the single family attached home category. The team developed a high-performance townhome design that fits in to urban settings. Ryerson University was also one of the winners in the best presentation category.

Image: Courtesy of Ryerson University

4 of 10

Montage Builders Northern Forest

The Montage Builders Northern Forest team from SUNY College of Environmental Science and Forestry, Syracuse University, and Onondaga Community College, won the grand award in the single family detached category for their craftsman-style, sustainable design. Pictured are Building Technologies Office Chief Architect Sam Rashkin (from left), team members Brent Crump, Peter LaCongo, Andrew Kenneally, Michelle Tinner, David Wallace, Gamika Korale, Jacek Bartczak, and Housing Innovation Research Laboratory Vice President of Engineering and Research Thomas Kenney.

Image: Dennis Schroeder, National Renewable Energy Laboratory

5 of 10

WINNING DESIGN

The Montage Builders Northern Forest team won the grand award in the single family detached home category. The team developed an accessible craftsman-style design that embodied responsible development. Montage Builders Northern Forest was also one of four winners in the best design solution category.

Image: Courtesy of Montage Builders Northern Forest
