National Clean Energy Business Plan Competition: 2014 Regional Winners

Addthis

1 of 5

Western Southwest Region Winner

KAir Battery won the Rice Business Plan Competition for its cost-effective large-scale stationary potassium-air battery.

Image: Courtesy of KAir Battery

2 of 5

Southeastern Region Winner

Energy Internet won the ACC Clean Energy Challenge for its software system that addresses cyber and control challenges facing the power grid.

Image: Courtesy of Georgia Institute of Technology

3 of 5

Eastern Midwest Region Winner

Black Pine Engineering won the Clean Energy Trust Clean Energy Challenge for its advanced turbomachinery system for geothermal technologies.

Image: Courtesy of Black Pine Engineering

4 of 5

Western Midwest Region

Superior Ecotech won the University of Colorado Cleantech New Ventures Challenge for its technology that uses algae to convert carbon dioxide waste into omega-3 oils and other useful products.

Image: Courtesy of Superior Ecotech

5 of 5

Northeast Region Winner

Unified Solar won the MIT Clean Energy Prize for its integrated circuit that reduces energy loss for solar panels.

Image: Courtesy of Unified Solar
