Past Workshops and Events

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) hosts workshops, conferences, roundtables, and other meetings related to solid-state lighting (SSL) to provide a forum for information exchange and updates on this rapidly evolving technology.

FEBRUARY 2017

DOE SSL R&D Workshop
Around 250 attendees gathered in Long Beach, CA, to participate in the 14th annual DOE SSL R&D Workshop, to share the latest on SSL advances, examine market forces that are shaping SSL technology, and explore how the technology is defining new market opportunities. Attendees also had an opportunity to provide input that will guide updates to the DOE SSL R&D Plan and DOE SSL funding solicitations.

Presentations and Materials

NOVEMBER 2016

DOE SSL Technology Development Workshop
More than 180 attendees participated in the 11th annual DOE Solid-State Lighting Technology Development Workshop in Denver, an event where government and industry converge to to address what needs to be done to leverage the full potential of SSL and tap even deeper energy savings.

DOE Connected Lighting Systems Workshop
