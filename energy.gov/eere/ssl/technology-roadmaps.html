Technology Roadmaps

This page contains links to DOE's Technology Roadmaps, multi-year plans outlining solid-state lighting goals, research and development initiatives aimed at accelerating technology advances and market penetration of solid-state lighting, and recent achievements. The following documents are available as Adobe Acrobat PDFs. 

  • OLED Stakeholder Meeting Report
    Summary of an October meeting open to members of the U.S. OLED lighting community with the purpose of creating an opportunity for open discussion concerning the development of OLED lighting, namely to identify critical R&D needs and market challenges. (30 pages, December 2016)

  • DOE Joint Solid-State Lighting Roundtables on Science Challenges 
    Summary of a September meeting of LED experts to consider opportunities for further advancement of SSL technology through coordinated R&D actions, convened by the DOE Office of Basic Energy Sciences and the DOE EERE SSL program. (20 pages, October 2016)
     
  • LED Product Development and Manufacturing R&D Roundtable Report 
    Summary of a September meeting of LED experts to identify critical topic areas for the SSL Program’s Product Development and Manufacturing R&D initiatives and to discuss the most critical technology challenges facing the industry today. (18 pages, October 2016)
     
  • Connected Lighting Systems Meetings Report
    Input and discussions from DOE workshops with lighting and IT industry stakeholders to identify critical challenges and accelerate development of connected lighting systems. (32 pages, October 2016)
     
  • 2016 Human Physiological Responses to Light Meeting Report
    Provides a summary of the input and subsequent discussions during a meeting, held in July 2016, on common research themes, research challenges, and paths forward to better understand the broad topic human physiological responses to light. (16 pages, September 2016)
     
  • 2016 Animal Responses to Light Meeting Report
    Provides a summary of the input and subsequent discussions during a meeting, held in April 2016, on common research themes, research challenges, and paths forward to better understand the broad topic of animal responses to light. (15 pages, June 2016)
     
  • 2016 Solid-State Lighting R&D Plan
    Provides analysis and direction for ongoing R&D activities to advance SSL technology and increase energy savings, reviewing SSL technology status and trends for both LEDs and OLEDs and offering an overview of the current DOE SSL R&D project portfolio. (208 pages, June 2016)
    • OLED Stakeholder Meeting Report
      Summary of a September meeting open to members of the U.S. OLED lighting community with the purpose of creating an opportunity for open discussion concerning the development of OLED lighting, namely to identify critical R&D needs and market development challenges. (27 pages, December 2015)
    • LED Core Technology R&D Roundtable Report
      Summary of a September meeting of LED experts to identify critical topic areas for the SSL Program’s Core Technology R&D initiatives and to discuss the most critical technology challenges facing the industry today. (19 pages, November 2015)
    • LED Product Development and Manufacturing R&D Roundtable Report
      Summary of a September meeting of LED experts to identify critical topic areas for the SSL Program’s Product Development and Manufacturing R&D initiatives and to discuss the most critical technology challenges facing the industry today. (17 pages, November 2015)
       
  • 2015 Solid-State Lighting R&D Plan

    • OLED Stakeholder Meeting Report
      Summary of an October meeting open to members of the U.S. OLED lighting community to share updates on the newly formed OLED Coalition’s efforts to advance OLED lighting and market viability, and to encourage participation in the Coalition, and to identify critical core, product development, and manufacturing R&D needs for the continued advancement of OLEDs. (23 pages, December 2014)
    • LED Product Development and Manufacturing R&D Roundtable Summary
      Summary of an October meeting of LED experts to identify critical topic areas for the SSL Program’s Product Development and Manufacturing R&D initiatives and to discuss the most critical technology challenges facing the industry today. (23 pages, December 2014)

(127 pages, August 2014)
    • Solid-State Lighting LED Manufacturing Roundtable Summary

(5 pages, February 2014)
     
  • DOE Roundtable on OLED Lighting Industry Planning

(14 pages, October 2013)

  • Basic Research Needs for Solid-State Lighting
    Report from DOE's Basic Energy Sciences Workshop on SSL, May 2006.  Examines the gap separating current state-of-the-art SSL technology from an energy-efficient, high-quality and economical SSL technology suitable for general illumination, and identifies the most significant fundamental scientific challenges and research directions that would enable that gap to be bridged (228 pages, September 2006)
     
  • Solid-State Lighting R&D Multi-Year Program Plan - July 2006 Update
    Provides an update to the Solid-State Lighting R&D Multi-Year Program Plan sections 3.0, 3.1 and 3.2 which describes the SSL funding mechanisms, and the current project portfolio. This update includes all awards up through July 2006. (7 pages, August 2006)
     
  • Solid-State Lighting R&D Multi-Year Program Plan

  • 2005 Solid-State Lighting Program Planning Workshop Report
    Report from DOE's February 2005 program planning workshop to review and reprioritize DOE's SSL R&D portfolio. Summary of presentations and output from breakout discussion groups. (99 pages, April 2005)
     
  • Illuminating the Challenges: Solid-State Lighting Program Planning Workshop Report
    Report from DOE's November 2003 program planning workshop to review and reprioritize DOE's SSL R&D portfolio. Summary of presentations and output from break-out discussion groups on LEDs and OLEDs. (73 pages, February 2004)
     
  • Fundamental Research Needs in Organic Electronic Materials
    Executive summary of May 2003 workshop to identify key scientific issues enabling the technological success of this class of materials. Jointly sponsored by DOE Basic Energy Sciences and Office of Energy Efficiency and Renewable Energy, Building Technologies Office. The full workshop report will be published in the Journal of Synthetic Materials. (5 pages, May 2003)
     
  • Light Emitting Diodes (LEDs) for General Illumination, Update 2002, An OIDA Technology Roadmap
    Updated SSL roadmap for LEDs provides quantitative enumeration of key technology targets, prioritization of research challenges, and key decisions related to competing approaches. Also discusses lighting systems issues necessary to achieve mass penetration of LEDs in the marketplace. Co-sponsored by DOE, NEMA, and OIDA. (112 pages, November 2002)
     
  • Organic Lighting Emitting Diodes (OLEDs) for General Illumination, Update 2002, A OIDA Technology Roadmap
    Updated SSL roadmap for OLEDs builds on the 2000 workshop, capturing updates from a second workshop in 2002 as well as some new information and detail on OLEDs. Co-sponsored by DOE and OIDA. (80 pages, August 2002)
     
  • The Promise of Solid-State Lighting for General Illumination
    SSL technology roadmap summarizes findings from an October 2000 LED workshop and a November 2000 OLED workshop. Co-sponsored by DOE and OIDA. (31 pages, 2001)
