SOLAR ENERGY RESOURCE CENTER

The SunShot Initiative’s Solar Energy Resource Center contains work developed by the Department of Energy, national laboratories and SunShot awardees. The Solar Energy Resource Center has over 100 unique documents which provide information on model standards and codes, utility policies, solar facilities, financing, incentives, market analysis, solar basics manufacturing, workforce development, shared solar, planning, zoning, permitting, and interconnection.

Codes, Standards & Utility Policies

Resources for regulators, utility managers, and legislators looking for solar policy examples that have been implemented across the country. 

Facilities & Buildings

Legal, tax, and financial information regarding municipal PV systems and policies and practices for building managers.  
 

Solar Basics & Educating Consumers

Checklists, consumer guides and other solar energy information targeted to individual consumers who are interested in going solar.

Financing, Incentives and Market Analysis

Information on solar financing options to help lower the cost of installing solar panels.

Manufacturing, Workforce & Training Resources

Solar job training, certification and workforce development resources for solar providers.
 

Shared Solar: Community & Solarize

Model programs, tool kits and other community solar resources to help launch shared solar programs across the nation.

System Performance

Evaluations and studies on improving concentrating solar power and photovoltaic system performance, interconnection and co-generation facilities.
 

Planning, Zoning, Permitting & Interconnection

Plans and model ordinances that help make solar siting a priority
