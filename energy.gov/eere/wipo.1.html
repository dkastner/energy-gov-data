Advancing Clean Energy With State and Local Governments

The Weatherization and Intergovernmental Programs Office strategically coordinates with state and local leaders to accelerate the adoption of energy efficiency and renewable energy technologies and best practices. These partnerships help American communities, businesses, and industries overcome barriers to a viable clean energy economy.
