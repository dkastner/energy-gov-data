State and Local Solution Center

  • New Guide Summarizes DOE Resources for Leveraging Federal Renewable Energy Tax Credits

    A new DOE guide outlines the wide array of resources available to state and local governments interested in leveraging federal tax credits to accelerate the deployment of clean, renewable energy.

    Read more
  • New LBNL Report Released on Current Practices in Efficiency Financing

    The Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory report helps guide customer-facing financing products—products offered by a lender directly to a borrower—used to pay for energy efficiency.

    Read more
  • Updated Best Practice Guidelines for Residential PACE Financing Released

    The guidelines provide best practices that can help state and local governments, PACE program administrators, and their partners to plan and implement programs that effectively deliver clean energy, water efficiency, and related upgrades to consumers.

    Read more
  • Better Buildings Toolkit Features Best Practices, Resources, and More

    The Energy Data Accelerator Toolkit is a collection of resources featured in the Better Buildings Solution Center. It describes best practices that enabled cities, utilities, and other stakeholders to overcome whole-building data access barriers.

    Read more
  • New Better Buildings Implementation Models

    Learn about how states and school districts are creating new energy efficiency programs, initiatives, and plans. New implementation models include: State of Rhode Island, State of New York, City of Rochester, City of Philadelphia, Portland Public Schools, and Camas School District.
