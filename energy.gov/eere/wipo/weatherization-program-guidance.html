Weatherization Program Guidance

The U.S. Department of Energy's (DOE) Weatherization Assistance Program (WAP) is governed by various federal regulations designed to help manage and account for the resources provided by DOE. Each year, Congress passes a Weatherization Assistance Program Appropriation.
