Worker Safety and Health Training at Oak Ridge Operations Office on March 13 - 16, 2017

January 17, 2017 - 11:40am

Addthis

Worker Safety and Health Program Implementation Assistance – Worker Safety and Health Training at the Oak Ridge Operations Office: 

On March 13 - 16, the Office of Worker Safety and Health Policy will provide OSHA 30-hour Construction Safety outreach training to 25 individuals at the Oak Ridge Operations Office.  The purpose of the training is to provide an enhanced understanding of the requirements of OSHA construction safety standards pursuant to DOE adoption of OSHA standards under 10 CFR 851 and DOE O 440.1B. These standards are the foundation of an effective site safety and health program as required by 10 CFR 851.
