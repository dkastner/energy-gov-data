Leadership

Susan M. Cange
Acting Assistant Secretary, Office of Environmental Management

Ms. Sue Cange is currently the Acting Assistant Secretary for Environmental Management. In this role she enables the overall EM mission and assists the Assistant Secretary with carrying out program and policy direction under EM’s jurisdiction. She ensures coordination of corporate initiatives across the EM complex, provides oversight of programs, and ensures overall program integration and operations to maintain line management accountability.

More about Acting Assistant Secretary for Environmental Management Cange
Stacy Charboneau
Associate Principal Deputy Assistant Secretary, Field Operations

Ms. Stacy Charboneau is currently the Associate Principal Deputy Secretary for Field Operations. The office provides leadership and develops mission strategies, policy, and guidance for field operations to support EM’s mission.

More about Associate Principal Deputy Assistant Secretary Charboneau
Frank Marcinowski
Associate Principal Deputy Assistant Secretary, Regulatory and Policy Affairs

Mr. Frank Marcinowski is currently the Associate Principal Deputy Secretary for Regulatory and Policy Affairs. The office provides leadership on matters related to EM’s legal and regulatory affairs defined by law and negotiated or stipulated compliance agreements, DOE policies, orders, and standards.

More about Associate Principal Deputy Assistant Secretary Marcinowski
Candice Trummell
Associate Principal Deputy Assistant Secretary, Corporate Services

Ms. Candice Trummell is currently the Associate Principal Deputy Secretary for Corporate Services. The office provides leadership and develops mission strategies, policy, and guidance for business services in support of EM’s environmental cleanup mission.
