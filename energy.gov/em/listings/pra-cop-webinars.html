P&RA CoP Webinars

Topics of interest to the CoP have been identified with the help of the steering committee, and will be discussed in quarterly Webinars. 

List of Topics for Interagency Performance & Risk Assessment Community of Practice (P&RA CoP) Discussion

May 16, 2016 Webinar - Predicting the Service Life of Geomembranes in Low-Level and Mixed-Waste Disposal Facilities

Performance & RIsk Assessment (P&RA) Community of Practice (CoP) Webinar - May 16, 2016 - Predicting the Service Life of Geomembranes in Low-Level and Mixed-Waste Disposal Facilities: Findings from a Long-Term Study. Presented by Dr. Craig Benson (Dean of School of Engineering and Applied Science, and Janet Scott Hamilton and John Downman Hamilton Professor, Univ. of Virginia).

February 23, 2016 Webinar - Multi-Criteria Decisional Analyses: Methodology and Case Studies

Performance & RIsk Assessment (P&RA) Community of Practice (CoP) Webinar - February 23, 2016 - Multi-Criteria Decisional Analyses: Methodology and Case Studies (Dr. Igor Linkov and Mr. Matthew Bates, U.S. Army Corps of Engineers).

January 28, 2016 Webinar - Borehole Disposal of Spent Radioactive Sources

Performance & RIsk Assessment (P&RA) Community of Practice (CoP) Webinar - January 28, 2016 - Borehole Disposal of Spent Radioactive Sources (Dr. Matt Kozak, INTERA).

November 12, 2015 Webinar - Implementing Optimization in the Superfund Program

P&RA CoP Webinar - November 12, 2015 Webinar - Implementing Optimization in the Superfund Program, Mr. Kirby Biggs and Mr. Daniel Powell (EPA OSRTI).

November 10, 2015 Webinar - Congressionally Mandated Review of the Use Of Risk-Informed Management in the DOE EM Program

P&RA CoP Webinar - November 10, 2015 Webinar - Congressionally Mandated Review of the Use Of Risk-Informed Management in the DOE EM Program. (Dr. Michael Greenberg, Rutgers University; Dr. Steven Krahn, Vanderbilt University; and Mr. Timothy Fields, MDB, Inc.).

October 13, 2015 Webinar - EPA Radiation Risk Assessment Approach

P&RA CoP Webinar - October 13, 2015 Webinar - EPA Radiation Risk Assessment Approach by Mr. Stuart Walker, U.S. Environmental Protection Agency, 2-3 pm Eastern Time.

August 18, 2015 Webinar - Probabilistic Analysis of Inadvertent Intrusion and the International Atomic Energy Agency Human Intrusion in the Context of Disposal of Radioactive Waste (HIDRA) Project

P&RA CoP Webinar - August 18, 2015 - Probabilistic Analysis of Inadvertent Intrusion and the International Atomic Energy Agency Human Intrusion in the Context of Disposal of Radioactive Waste (HIDRA) Project, by Dr. Paul Black (Neptune) and Mr. Roger Seitz (Savannah River National Laboratory), August 18, 2015, 1:30 – 3:00 pm Eastern Daylight Time.

May 20, 2015 Webinar - Guidance for Conducting Technical Analyses for 10 CFR Part 61

P&RA CoP Webinar - May 20, 2015 - Guidance for Conducting Technical Analyses for 10 CFR Part 61 by Mr. Chris Grossman (NRC)

October 16, 2014 Webinar - Decisional Analysis under Uncertainty

Webinar October 16, 2014, 11 am – 12:40 pm EDT: Dr. Paul Black (Neptune, Inc), Decisional Analysis under Uncertainty

June 3, 2014 Webinar - Features, Events, and Processes: Practical Considerations for Development and Selection of Scenarios

P&RA CoP Webinar - June 3, 2014 - Features, Events, and Processes: Practical Considerations for Development and Selection of Scenarios
Geoff Freeze (SNL) and Roger Seitz (SRNL)

February 20, 2014 Webinar - Performance of Engineered Barriers: Lessons Learned

P&RA CoP Webinar - 2/20/2014 - Performance of Engineered Barriers: Lessons Learned
Craig H. Benson (University of Wisconsin-Madison/CRESP)

February 5, 2014 Webinar - The Cementitious Barriers Partnership Toolbox, Version 2.0

P&RA CoP Webinar - February 5, 2014 - Tools and Capabilities of the Cementitious Barriers Partnership Toolbox, Version 2.0
David Kosson et al. (Vanderbilt University/CRESP)

December 12, 2013 Webinar - The Use of Graded Approach in Hanford Vadose Zone Modeling

P&RA CoP Webinar - Dec. 12, 2013 - Alaa Aly (INTERA) & Dib Goswami (Washington State Ecology), “The Use of Graded Approach in Hanford Vadose Zone Modeling”
