November 18, 2015
How Does the WIPP Shutdown Impact New Mexico, Idaho, and South Carolina

Southwest Research and Information Center presented on the Impact of the WIPP shutdown on the states of New Mexico, Idaho, and South Carolina, the presenter was Don Hancock. Information provided covered: budget numbers, waste storage numbers, safety, and what needs to be done to reopen WIPP.

November 18, 2015
Treatment of Remediated Nitrate Salts

Los Alamos National Laboratory, provided a presentation at the NNMCABs November 18, 2015 Board Meeting at New Mexico Highlands University. The topic of the presentation was the plan for remediation the nitrate salt waste from the 3706 campaign that is currently stored at Material Disposal Area G, presenter was David Funk, LANS.
