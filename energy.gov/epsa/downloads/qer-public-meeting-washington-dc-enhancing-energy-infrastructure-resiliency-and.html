10:00 A.M.. to 5:00 P.M.. EST

East Capitol Street, N.E. and First Street N.E. Washington, D.C. 20001

Regarding Public Comments

The entirety of all comments received in response to the Quadrennial Energy Review will become publicly available.  Any personally identifiable information, such as names, addresses, and email addresses, that may be included in a comment will automatically be made available to the public.  Individual commenters may request that the Department withhold their personally identifiable information from public disclosure, and such requests will be honored to the extent allowable by law. If you wish for the Department to withhold your name and/or other personally identifiable information, please state this prominently at the beginning of your comment.  Additionally, the Department will accept comments submitted anonymously.

1 - The transcript of the April 11, 2014 QER public meeting has not been edited by the U.S. Department of Energy.
