SEMI-ANNUAL REPORTING REQUIREMENTS (LNG EXPORTERS) 2010-2016 DOCKETS

Companies with authorizations to export LNG are required to file, on a semi-annual basis, written reports describing the progress of the planned liquefaction facility project that is part of the company’s LNG export application and order.  These reports are due by April 1 and October 1 of each year, and shall include the following:

A description of the progress of the planned LNG terminal and/or liquefaction facility
The date the facility is expected to be operational
The status of the long-term contracts associated with the long-term export of LNG
The status of any long-term supply contracts

Semi-annual reports should be e-mailed to Fergas@hq.doe.gov.

Please select each Applicant to view the report(s). 

 

* Semi-Annual Report submitted for other dockets covering the same terminal. 

All other approved dockets on our current list were approved after October 1, 2014, and thus are not obligated to file prior to April, 2015.
