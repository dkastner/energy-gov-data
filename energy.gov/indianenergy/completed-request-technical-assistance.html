Completed On-Request Technical Assistance

The U.S. Department of Energy Office of Indian Energy provides federally recognized Indian tribes, including Alaska Native villages, tribal energy resource development organizations, and other organized tribal groups and communities, with technical assistance to advance tribal energy projects. The map and table below capture all completed on-request technical assistance since 2013. Filter the table by tribe, state, year, topic, or technical area, or use the search box.
