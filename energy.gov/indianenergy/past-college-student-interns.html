Past College Student Interns

Since 2002, the U.S. Department of Energy college student internship program has provided more than 30 Native American undergraduate and graduate students with first-hand experience working on tribal renewable energy projects and direct exposure to issues concerning tribal renewable energy technology use and application. Find details about the interns and download their research papers below. Search the table below or sort by clicking on the headers for student name, tribe, degree, year, or research paper.
