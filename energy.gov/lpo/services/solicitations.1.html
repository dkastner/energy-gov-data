Solicitations

The Section 1703 is currently accepting applications through its Advanced Fossil Energy Projects Solicitation and the Renewable Energy and Efficient Energy Projects Solicitation. The LPO is also still evaluating previously submitted applications for other technology areas. The ATVM loan program continues to accept applications on a rolling basis.
