Requests for LPO Speakers and/or Meetings

  • Congressional and press requests should be processed through the normal channels.
  • If you would like an LPO official to speak about the LPO or the Fossil Solicitation at an event you are hosting, please provide details of that event to LPO.FossilSolicitation.Speeches@hq.doe.gov.
  • If you would like to meet with an LPO official regarding the Fossil Solicitation, please provide details (including a proposed agenda and a list of all companies to be represented at the meeting, the individuals proposed to attend, and such individuals’ titles) to LPO.FossilSolicitation.Meetings@hq.doe.gov.
  • NOTES:
    NO SPECIFIC PROJECTS OR POTENTIAL APPLICATIONS WILL BE DISCUSSED OR OTHERWISE ADDRESSED
    ALL PRESENTATIONS, SPEECHES, COMMENTS, QUESTIONS, RESPONSES, AND THE CONTENT OF ALL DISCUSSIONS MAY BE MADE PUBLIC ON THIS WEBSITE AND ELSEWHERE
