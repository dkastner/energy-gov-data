Emergency Preparedness

DOE has established emergency procedures to respond to incidents affecting DOE HQ facilities, personnel, and/or operations in the National Capital Region to ensure the safety and security of its employees, guests, and visitors. On this page is a collection of documents

  • Emergency Procedures Pamphlets
  • Building Evacuation Routes
  • Occupant Emergency Plans (OEP's)

All of these documents are only available to computers attached to the DOE Network.  They are for use only by DOE Headquarters employees.  Any computer not on the DOE Network will get an error message when trying to access one of the documents.

Headquarters Emergency Procedures Pamphlets

  • Forrestal Emergency Procedures (pdf) Updated September, 2015 - DOE Internal Access Only
  • Germantown Emergency Procedures (pdf) Updated September, 2015 - DOE Internal Access Only
  • Portals III Emergency Procedures (pdf) March, 2016 - DOE Internal Access Only

Pamphlets for the Corporate 270 Building, Reporters Building, 950 L’Enfant facility, and 955 L’Enfant facility are currently being revised and will be posted when completed.

Building Evacuation Routes and Assembly Areas

Posted here are the building evacuation routes of DOE Headquarters' buildings, and their Assembly Areas.  The Forrestal and Germantown diagrams are generic floor plans that do not represent any one area and can be used for identifying your specific evacuation route and posting on the back of your door. The 950 and 955 L'Enfant Plaza files have floor-specific diagrams. All questions go to 202-586-9537/7949/7959.

  • Forrestal Floor Evacuation Routes/Assembly Areas (pdf) Updated December, 2014 - DOE Internal Access Only
  • Germantown Floor Evacuation Routes/Assembly Areas (pdf) Updated December, 2014 - DOE Internal Access Only
  • Portals III 5th, 6th, and 7th Floor Evacuation Routes/Assembly Areas (pdf) September, 2015 - DOE Internal Access Only
  • 950 L'Enfant Plaza 6th, 7th and 8th Floor Evacuation Routes (pdf) Updated November, 2014 - DOE Internal Access Only
  • 955 L'Enfant Plaza Floor Evacuation Routes (pdf) - currently being revised and will be posted when completed
  • Corporate 270 Building 3rd and 4th Floor Evacuation Routes/Assembly Areas (pdf) Updated October, 2016 - DOE Internal Access Only
  • Reporters Building Floor Evacuation Routes - currently being revised and will be posted when completed
