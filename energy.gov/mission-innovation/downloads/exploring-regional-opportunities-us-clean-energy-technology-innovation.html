Exploring Regional Opportunities in the U.S. for Clean Energy Technology Innovation - Volume II: University Reports, October 2016

This is Volume II of a two-volume report, “Exploring Regional Opportunities in the U.S. for Clean Energy Technology Innovation”.  Volume I of the report provides a summary of what was learned, and Volume II provides an account of the proceedings from the individual university forums, with the aim of sharing ideas and lessons learned across regions and among policy makers.
