Welcome to the DOE NEPA Website

The DOE NEPA Website serves as a focal point for DOE National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA) implementation and contains information about current DOE NEPA activities, an electronic archive of DOE NEPA documents, and other resources for NEPA practitioners and members of the public, including requirements, guidance, and schedules.

For further information about the DOE NEPA process, to provide comments and suggestions to make this Website more useful, or for assistance in locating material, please contact us.
