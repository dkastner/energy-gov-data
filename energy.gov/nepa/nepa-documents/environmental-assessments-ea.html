Environmental Assessments (EA)

Environmental Assessments are concise public documents that a Federal agency prepares under NEPA to provide sufficient evidence and analysis to determine whether a proposed agency action would require preparation of an environmental impact statement or a finding of no significant impact.

If you have any trouble finding a specific document, please contact AskNEPA@hq.doe.gov for assistance.
