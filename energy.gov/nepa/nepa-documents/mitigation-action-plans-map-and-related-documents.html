Mitigation Action Plans (MAP) and Related Documents

Mitigation Action Plans are documents DOE prepares in accordance with DOE NEPA regulations (10 CFR 1021.331) that describes the plan for implementing commitments made in a DOE environmental impact statement and its associated record of decision, or, when appropriate, an EA or FONSI, to mitigate adverse environmental impacts associated with an action.

If you have any trouble finding a specific document, please contact AskNEPA@hq.doe.gov for assistance.
