Leadership

Christy L. Jackiewicz
Deputy Director

Christy L. Jackiewicz assumed office on January 9, 2017 as the Deputy Director for the Office of Small and Disadvantaged Business Utilization. She leads an office established to advocate in favor of small businesses looking to contract with the Department of Energy. Drawing on nearly three decades of public service at the General Services Administration, Jackiewicz brings a diverse background and capable skillset to DOE.

More about Christy L. Jackiewicz
Camille J. George
Chief Business Officer, Office of Small and Disadvantaged Business Utilization

Camille George is the Chief Business Officer for the Office of Small and Disadvantaged Business Utilization at the U.S. Department of Energy and charged with ensuring the strategic goals, daily business objectives, and priorities of the OSDBU are achieved.

More about Camille J. George
