January 19, 2017
OSDBU FY 2015 Annual Report Summary Final

The U.S. Department of Energy’s (DOE) Office of Small and Disadvantaged Business Utilization (OSDBU) has been working on behalf of DOE’s small business partners since 1978 to make federal procurement more readily accessible to small businesses. OSDBU is focused on building sustainable small businesses to enable DOE to achieve our mission through innovation, creativity, and efficiency—all hallmarks of small businesses. DOE and OSDBU have made significant strides in engaging and empowering small businesses.

January 27, 2016
BES Technologies Wins CNS-Y12 Small Business Award

October 2015: BES Technologies (BEST) recently received a Small Business Award from Consolidated Nuclear Security (CNS), the Y-12 contractor for the Department of Energy in Oak Ridge, TN. The award was made from the Socioeconomic Programs Office, and other awardees included K-9 Search on Site; Secured Transportation Services, LLC; Personal Computer Systems, Inc.; and Container Technologies Industries, LLC. BEST performs radioactive water, laundry and respirator services.

The U.S. Department of Energy’s Idaho National Laboratory reached out to small business owners throughout the state recently by meeting individually and in groups to explain the national laboratory’s needs for achieving its research missions.
