DOE EM 2015 Year in Review - Paducah Site

The U.S. Department of Energy's Office of Environmental Management (EM) has published its 2015 Year in Review, outlining the major accomplishments achieved over the course of this year at the Paducah Gaseous Diffusion Plant Site and throughout the EM program. The Paducah Site section is available by clicking here or on the link below. The EM-Wide Review is available at: http://energy.gov/em/downloads/department-energy-office-environmental-management-2015-year-review.
