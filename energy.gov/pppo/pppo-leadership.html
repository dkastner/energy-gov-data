PPPO Leadership

Robert E. Edwards, III
Manager

Mr. Robert Edwards was appointed Manager of the Department of Energy’s Portsmouth/Paducah Project Office (PPPO) on July 24, 2016. He joined PPPO as Deputy Manager on December 30, 2012. Located in Lexington, Kentucky, PPPO is charged with the environmental remediation, deactivation, and decontamination and decommissioning of the former gaseous diffusion plants near Paducah, KY and Portsmouth, OH.

More about Mr. Edwards
Joel B. Bradburne
Portsmouth Site Lead

Mr. Joel B. Bradburne joined PPPO in June 2009 as Site Lead for the Portsmouth Site. Mr. Bradburne’s 27 years of experience include shipyard, commercial nuclear, U.S. Department of Energy (DOE), and foreign nuclear site decommissioning in various program- and project-management assignments. Mr. Bradburne's DOE experience includes 14 years at various DOE sites and projects throughout the nation.

More about Mr. Bradburne
Jennifer Woodard
Paducah Site Lead

Jennifer Woodard began her current assignment as the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) Paducah Site Lead on June 15, 2014. Mrs. Woodard has more than 20 years of experience in engineering and program/project management with DOE. As a federal official at the Paducah Site for the past six years, Mrs. Woodard has served in multiple capacities, including Federal Facility Agreement Manager (FFA), Alternate Deputy Designated Federal Official to the Citizens' Advisory Board, Project Manager, and Contracting Officer's Representative.
