This Year, the Department recognized eight (8) individual and team successes in project management. Award presentations were made by Elizabeth Sherwood-Randall, Deputy Secretary of Energy.

  • Office of Project Management Oversight & Assessments (PM)

    Provides oversight, managerial leadership, and assistance in the development and implementation of Department-wide policies, procedures, programs and management systems pertaining to project management and related activities.

  • December 2016 DOE Project Management Workforce News

    Click here to read the latest interactive edition of DOE Project Management News. This month's edition of the Program Management Workforce Newsletter includes the Director's Corner and features articles on a recent peer review with reflections from the participating FPD, news FPDs can use regarding planned Real Property scope, a sneak preview of the PARS IIe system, and a invitation to visit our new robust DOE Project Management site – PM-MAX – and offer your feedback and comments about the site. This issue also includes information on upcoming PMCDP classroom and online training, as well as the list of recently certified FPDs. Don't forget to make your plans to attend the PM Workshop in March 2017. Registration is now open.
