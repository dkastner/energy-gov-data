Energy Economy

The clean energy economy continues to grow, creating new job opportunities for tens of thousands of Americans along the way. Learn more about the growth of America's clean energy economy. | Infographic by Sarah Gerrity, Energy Department.

Investments in clean energy technologies create jobs and grow the national economy. From funding and financing opportunities, to workforce education and training, to analysis of energy policy and systems, the Energy Department is leading the charge to create more clean energy jobs by building partnerships that drive workforce development across a variety of proven and next-era technologies.
